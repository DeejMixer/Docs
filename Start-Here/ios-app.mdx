## Download

1. Open **App Store** on iPhone or iPad
2. Search **"Mixlar Control"**
3. Tap **Get** to download
4. Open the app

## Connect

### Local Network (Same WiFi)

**Automatic:**
1. Tap **Local Network**
2. Your PC should appear automatically
3. Tap your PC's name
4. Enter username and password
5. Tap **Connect**

**Manual:**
1. Tap **Enter Manually**
2. Enter `http://YOUR_PC_IP:5000`
3. Enter credentials
4. Tap **Connect**

### Remote (Tailscale)

1. Install Tailscale on your iPhone
2. Sign in with same account as PC
3. In Mixlar Control app, tap **Tailscale**
4. Enter your Tailscale URL (from PC's Network tab)
5. Enter credentials
6. Tap **Connect**

## Using the App

**Mixer Tab:**
- Control 4 sliders
- Adjust master volume
- Mute buttons

**Macros Tab:**
- 36 macro buttons (4 pages)
- Swipe left/right to change pages
- Tap to execute

**Settings Tab:**
- Manage connections
- App preferences
- View connection status

## Troubleshooting

**Can't find PC:**
- Both on same WiFi?
- Web Controller running?
- Try manual connection

**Tailscale not working:**
- Tailscale running on both devices?
- Same account?
- Correct URL from Network tab?

**Login fails:**
- Check credentials in PC settings
- Passwords are case-sensitive
