## ğŸ§© Multi-Binding (Control Multiple Things at Once)

Mixlar allows you to bind **multiple items** to the same slider.

This is useful for:

- Grouping browser + music + video audio  
- Controlling all chat programs together  
- Pairing mic volume with noise suppression levels  
- Linking multiple smart devices to one slider  

Just select more than one item in the Assignment Window and press **Save**.
