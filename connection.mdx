# Connecting Your Mixlar Mix

Your Mixlar Mix can connect to Mixlar Control in two ways:

- **USB Type-C (recommended for first-time setup)**
- **Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for wireless mode**

Below are step-by-step instructions for both connection methods.

---

## üîå USB Type-C Connection

USB mode is the simplest and most reliable way to connect your Mixlar Mix.

### **1. Open the Device Settings**
Click the **gear icon** in the top-right corner of Mixlar Control.

<Image
  src="/images/45bd0bba-f344-40bd-800c-741a7234a60b.png"
  alt="Gear icon for accessing settings"
/>

### **2. Select the Device Tab**
From the left sidebar, choose **Device**.

<Image
  src="/images/d02730ac-e7ab-4c74-aa1a-f6b3352a832a.png"
  alt="Device settings and COM port list"
/>

### **3. Choose a COM Port**
Open the COM Port dropdown.

- Your COM port naming may differ depending on your computer  
- It is **usually not COM1**  
- Select each one until the mixer connects  

### **4. Click Connect**
Once connected, your Mixlar Mix will appear on the main screen.

<Image
  src="/images/a28ae6bd-341b-4c05-90b3-a9856759c86e.png"
  alt="Mixlar Mix main screen showing sliders"
/>

<Card icon="info-circle" title="Tip">
You can confirm USB mode by checking the **USB icon** on your Mixlar Mix home screen.
</Card>

## üì° Wireless (BLE) Connection

Bluetooth Low Energy allows your Mixlar Mix to be used **completely wirelessly**.  
Your PC **must** support BLE or you must use the **Mixlar BLE Dongle**.

### **Before You Begin**
- Keep the Mixlar Mix **plugged in** during pairing  
- Ensure the **Connections** page is open in Mixlar Control  
- Select the **Wireless** tab at the top  

<Image
  src="/images/Screenshot-2025-12-08-10-53-55-AM.png"
  alt="Wireless tab and BLE connection interface"
/>

---

## **Step-by-Step BLE Setup**

### **1. Pair via USB**
On the Wireless tab, click **Pair via USB**.

This securely links the software to your device before going wireless.

### **2. Unplug Your Mixer**
Disconnect the USB cable, then power the mixer using:

- The internal battery, **or**  
- An external USB power source (not connected to the PC)

### **3. Wait for the Connect Screen**
Your Mixlar Mix will show a **Connect** screen once it is ready.

### **4. Press Connect on the Mixer**
Tap the **Connect** button on the mixer display to begin wireless pairing.

Once connected, the device will appear in the list as **Mixlar Control XXXX**.
